About Us

Founded in 2018, Quality Exports is determined to forge its name amongst the leading firms promoting & providing top grades of environmentally friendly jute products. We are manufacturing modern styles and standards of products for regular home based, corporate based, traveling and other purposes. The line offered by us contains Designer Jute Folder, Brown Jute Backpack, Jute Envelope, Jute Handcrafted Coaster, Designer Jute Rug, Jute File Folder, Jute Conference Bag, Square Jute Tray and many more items. Our purpose is to spread our qualitative products from the local markets of Kolkata (West Bengal) to all across India and, become a trusted partner for customers in the domain.
